====== Hidden Step ======
Created Wednesday 04 December 2019

As a bonus action, you can magically turn invisible until the start of your next turn or until you attack, make a damage roll, or force someone to make a saving throw. Once you use this trait, you can't make a saving throw. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
